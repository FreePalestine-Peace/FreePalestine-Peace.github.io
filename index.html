Peaceful Resolutions for the Palestine-Israel Conflict and The Importance Protecting The Lives of the Innocent

Regardless of who is holds claim to the land of Palestine-Israel, As tensions arise between both countries it is important we continue to express the importance of protecting innocent lives to parties who are willing to sacrifice it in order to gain claim over worldy benefit. In order to do so, it is important to consider and present potential solutions to the conflict. The Israel-Palestine Conflict is a longstanding dispute over the land, with both sides claiming legitimate ownership. The Jewish people claim the land based on their religious connections and previous occupancy, while the Muslim people claim it based on their religious connection and occupancy of the land for the past 10 centuries. Despite this, it is important to focus on finding a solution to prevent further loss of life, rather than continuing to debate the ownership of the land.One potential solution is to draw on past native vs settler conflicts and their solutions, as well as current solutions presented by experts in the media. By combining this knowledge with internal and external perspectives, it may be possible to construct a solution to the conflict. Another potential solution to the conflict between Israel and Palestine is the two-state solution, which would involve the creation of an independent Palestinian state alongside the state of Israel. Another potential solution is the one-state solution, which would involve the creation of a single, bi-national state with equal rights and representation for Israelis and Palestinians. Another approach that has been suggested is the use of international mediation and peacekeeping forces to help facilitate negotiations and provide security in the region. Ultimately, finding a solution to the conflict will require willingness from both sides to compromise and work towards peace
